I am dyslexic.
Growing up, my dad would read Harry Potter to me because I couldn't do it myself.
He was my hero.
But without reading, I couldn't become the person I wanted to be.
So, I taught myself to code and built Speechify to be my text to speech platform.
Together with over 200 amazing people at Speechify, we've become the largest provider of AI speech in the world.
Today, over 55 million people can achieve their full potential in school and society because Speechify lets them read faster, read more, and remember more.
Thank you for using Speechify!
